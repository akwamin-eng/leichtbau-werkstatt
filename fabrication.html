Our Process

How a job moves
through the shop.

01
Inspection & Measurement
The car is stripped to bare metal where required. We map existing structure with a datum gauge and photograph every surface before a single cut is made.
02
Design & Material Selection
Depending on application — road restoration or full race build — we spec the correct alloy, wall thickness, and join geometry. We document this before we order steel.
03
Fabrication & Fitment
All cutting, bending, and welding happens in-house. Each piece is test-fitted before final weld. Roll cage nodes are verified geometrically before sign-off.
04
Inspection & Surface Prep
Welds are dressed, ground, and inspected. Structural pieces are epoxy-primed before any subsequent process (paint, underseal, or assembly) proceeds.
